# BL-610 — Stop live IG clips spilling to paid Apify

**Shipped 2026-07-21.** main `4f1279e` (merge). Tags: pre/post-BL-610, pre/post-merge-BL-610. Rollback: `git revert 4f1279e`.

## Problem (BL-608's finding, confirmed)
The surviving ~$1.16/day IG Apify spend is NOT dead clips and NOT cooldowns. HikerAPI is healthy (BL-577/608). Two paths sent clips HikerAPI could serve for free to **paid** Apify:
1. **Budget spill** — when the per-tick HikerAPI budget `TICK_HIKER_BUDGET_MS=200s` was exhausted mid-sweep, overflow URLs were pushed to `missingForApify` and paid Apify (`apify.ts` batch fan-out).
2. **Carousel rejection** — `hikerapi.ts` rejected **every** carousel (`verdict:"carousel"`), which then fell to the apidojo $0.01 **0-result** runs, even though BL-605 taught HikerAPI to read mixed carousels.

## Fix — PART 1 (budget spill)
- `HIKER_GLOBAL_CONCURRENCY` **10 → 15**. Provider-allowed rate is 15/s (BL-577); the semaphore bounds *in-flight* requests, so at ~2s/call, 15 in flight ≈ 7.5 req/s started, under the 15/s ceiling. 15 is the ceiling; never higher.
- `TICK_HIKER_BUDGET_MS` **200s → `TRACKING_LOOP_BUDGET_MS` (210s)** — the cron's single source of truth (300s maxDuration − 90s end-step reserve), so the two budgets can't drift and HikerAPI gets the whole loop window.
- **Over-budget overflow no longer escalates to paid Apify.** It is left **absent from `primaryMap`** (exactly like an un-started chunk): the clip resolves to `null` downstream → `tracking.ts` keeps last-known (**NULL never 0**, BL-543) → it is re-probed on the **next HikerAPI tick (~10 min)**. HikerAPI is prepaid + healthy, so waiting one tick is free.
- **Finite tick (no infinite loop):** the fan-out is `Promise.all` over a semaphore; no new lane starts once `Date.now()-tickStart > budget`, and each in-flight lane is bounded by `HIKER_TIMEOUT_MS = 25s`. Worst-case wall-clock ≤ 210s + 25s. The loop-level `deadlineAt` (`shouldStop` in `runWithLimit`) independently caps the tick the same safe way.

## Fix — PART 2 (carousel rejection)
- A **mixed carousel with a finite summed count > 0** (BL-605 `classifyV2Media` sum) is now a genuine **HikerAPI HIT** (`verdict:"ok"`, `useResult:true`, stats from the summed views) — served by HikerAPI, **never Apify**.
- A carousel HikerAPI **cannot count** (image-only, or mixed whose video children hide `play_count` → summed views null/≤0) is **QUARANTINED**: new `verdict:"quarantine"`, `useResult:false`, caller **SKIPS Apify** (apidojo returns 0-result on these too), keeps last-known (**NULL never 0**), re-probes next tick. Quarantined carousels are **not** added to `igGoneUrls` (alive, not gone — they never advance the dead-clip counter).
- `unknown`/opaque 200s still fall through to Apify as the **genuine-rescue floor**.
- Applied on **both** the batch fan-out and the single-fetch overlay.
