## What it does
A live calculator built INTO the campaign creation modal that turns the owner's model into exact numbers and the CPMs the campaign stores. New pure module `src/lib/campaign-money-calc.ts` runs every operation on `Prisma.Decimal` (decimal.js, via the browser-safe `@/generated/prisma/browser` entry so it is client-safe), NEVER float.

## The model (verbatim, not reinterpreted)
* **Marketed budget** is the headline clippers and clients see.
* **Platform fee %** (e.g. Whop 11%) is GHOST money: excluded from the pool, never a clipper line item.
* **Real money** = budget minus ghost fee.
* **Owner %** is taken from REAL money as a LOCKED share.
* **Clipper pool** is the REMAINDER of real money.
* **Payout fee %** is a PROJECTION on the pool (owner revenue as clippers withdraw), labelled an ESTIMATE, never deducted up front.
* A fully-earned pool reads as the MARKETED figure fully spent.

## Exactness (PART 1)
* **Rounding rule, stated + consistent:** dollars HALF_UP to the cent, CPMs HALF_UP to 4dp.
* **The clipper pool absorbs the remainder** (`pool = realMoney − ownerShare`, never rounded on its own), so `ghost + owner + pool` ALWAYS equals the marketed budget EXACTLY, no cent created or lost.
* **CPM agreement (BL-539/BL-570):** the calculator hands the campaign only two rate numbers, `clipperCpm` (the owner's target) and `ownerCpm = round(clipperCpm × s/(1−s), 4)`. POST `/api/campaigns` derives the stored share ITSELF as `ownerCpm/(ownerCpm+clipperCpm)` at creation (schema:582), so there is ONE source of the ratio (the two CPMs) and no independent locked-share value to drift from it. The owner's fraction of any clip's total is `ownerCpm/(ownerCpm+clipperCpm)` EXACTLY at every view count (the views/1000 factor cancels; a multiplication identity, no division, so decimal.js holds it exactly).
* **The dollar split uses the target owner % directly**, so it matches the owner's headline arithmetic to the cent. The result exposes BOTH the target % (drives the split) and the exact stored share the 4dp CPMs imply, so nothing is hidden.
