## PART 2 — why HikerAPI is not serving these (the leak, file:line)

The IG batch fan-out DOES call HikerAPI first (`tryHikerForInstagram`,
apify.ts:1458) and correctly SKIPS Apify on a definitive gone verdict
(apify.ts:1488-1494). Two structural reasons still push clips to Apify:

1. PER-TICK HIKERAPI TIME BUDGET (the top fix). `TICK_HIKER_BUDGET_MS = 200_000`
   (apify.ts:1421). Once a tick has spent 200s on HikerAPI, every remaining clip is
   `budgetAbandoned` and pushed to Apify WITHOUT HikerAPI being tried
   (apify.ts:1447-1448, 1478). HikerAPI is PREPAID and healthy, yet this cap diverts
   the batch overflow onto PAID Apify. Live clips -> apify/instagram-api-scraper
   (1 result); the abandoned clips also never get a gone verdict, which is one
   reason the counter under-fires.
2. CAROUSEL REJECTION. `tryHikerForInstagram` returns `useResult:false` verdict
   "carousel" for ANY carousel classification (hikerapi.ts:712) BEFORE its views
   check, so every IG carousel falls to Apify and burns an apidojo 0-result $0.01 —
   even though HikerAPI's own `classifyV2Media` can already read a mixed carousel
   (sum of video children) or correctly return null for image-only (BL-604/BL-605).

Count on the probed sample: HikerAPI RESOLVES-FINE = 16 of 19 (14/14 random +
2/5 gone-ordered); gone_404 = 3 of 19 (all from the gone-ordered sample);
genuine-rescue-only-Apify-can = 0 (matches BL-550's Apify 0-of-37 on gone IG). So
essentially everything Apify is being paid for, HikerAPI could have served for
free — the definition of a leak.

## PART 3 — retirement is working; there is simply little to retire

915 active IG clips still polling; `max(consecutiveGone) = 1`, 0 at >=3, 812 at
`checkIntervalMin >= 1440` (the cadence retirement candidates). 725 IG clips are
already retired (`videoUnavailable=true`); 2 were retired today. The retire-dead-clips
cron fires at 06:00 UTC (railway-cron-scheduler.ts:92 = 08:00 Belgrade) and its
candidate query keys on `consecutiveGone>=3 OR checkIntervalMin>=1440`
(retire-dead-clips.ts:75-77), so it is NOT blocked by the stuck counter — the 812
cadence candidates ARE eligible. It retires ONLY on a fresh HikerAPI 404
(retire-dead-clips.ts). Because the random sample shows the polled population is
~live, MOST cadence candidates re-probe ALIVE (200) and are correctly left polling;
only 2 were truly gone today. So retired_today=2 is CONSISTENT with a live
population, not evidence of a broken cron. The counter stuck at 1 (BL-588 persists)
is real but SECONDARY: with a live population it drives little spend, and the
cadence path is the working safety net.

## PART 5 — verdict + kill plan

VERDICT: HikerAPI is healthy and can serve essentially the entire active IG
population; today's Instagram Apify spend is almost all AVOIDABLE leakage of live +
carousel clips, not a genuine rescue floor and not a dead-clip retirement failure.

Dollar buckets (estimated from the run log + the 19-clip probe):
1. Confirmed-dead not yet retired: small (max_gone=1, ~0% gone in the random
   sample; ~2 retired/day is about right).
2. LIVE clips wrongly escalating past HikerAPI: the LARGE bucket — the per-tick
   HikerAPI budget overflow + carousel rejection.
3. Profile/bio calls: not in this log; small, separate.
4. Genuine rescue (only Apify can): ~0 (BL-550: Apify 0-of-37 gone).
5. TikTok: a few cents/day, the honest floor.

Ordered kill plan (build-ready spec; nothing built this round; keep NULL-never-0
per BL-543 in every path):
1. TOP FIX — raise or remove the per-tick HikerAPI budget and lift concurrency so
   the whole IG batch is served by HikerAPI, not spilled to Apify. Files:
   `TICK_HIKER_BUDGET_MS` (apify.ts:1421) and `HIKER_GLOBAL_CONCURRENCY`
   (apify.ts:1415/1858). HikerAPI is prepaid and answered 19/19 probes, so a hard
   200s cap that diverts overflow to PAID Apify is the primary leak. Raise the
   budget to cover a full tick (or gate the Apify fallback so a budget-abandoned
   clip is DEFERRED to the next tick's HikerAPI instead of paying Apify).
2. CAROUSEL FIX — stop routing IG carousels to Apify. Either (a) let
   `tryHikerForInstagram` USE `classifyV2Media`'s carousel result (mixed = video
   sum, image-only = null per BL-605) instead of rejecting at hikerapi.ts:712, or
   (b) since carousels have no paid campaign (CAROUSELS_DISABLED default on),
   quarantine/stop polling IG carousels so they never reach the paid apidojo tier.
   This kills the $0.01 apidojo 0-result runs.
3. SECONDARY — repair the counter feed so genuinely-dead IG clips increment past 1
   and retire promptly (BL-588 root cause), and consider raising retire-dead-clips
   `maxPerRun` above 100 if a real dead backlog ever appears. Low priority given the
   population is live.
4. Later + cheap — move the profile/bio verify calls off Apify to HikerAPI
   (BL-589), a separate ~$0.29/mo cut.
