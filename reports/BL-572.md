## The correct fix (design; needs BL-563 merged first, then a round scoped to the owner-lock)
The guard must sit at the **binding** site, the BL-163 owner-lock, not only 2145:
1. Merge BL-563 first (puts `decideOwnerGross` on main and refactors agency-monitor to it — one shared
   guard, no drift). This is BL-563's own round, not this one.
2. Compute the decision ONCE where the rates are in scope (near 2143, using `ownerCpmResolved`/`cCpm`

   clipperGross: breakdown.clipperEarnings, ownerCpm: ownerCpmResolved, clipperCpm: cCpm})`.
3. At the owner-lock (2557-2582): if the decision is `ambiguous`, set
   `newOwnerAmt = thisClipCurrentOwner` (the stored agency amount fetched at 2364-2365) instead of
   re-deriving. This PRESERVES the row (write at 2762 upserts the same value: a no-op; never zeroed) and
   feeds the budget accounting (2598) the correct stored figure. Guard 2145 the same way for the
   budget==0 edge. AGREE and NO-STAMP clips are byte-identical (same 2577 formula, `decideOwnerGross`
   returns `gross`). The clipper side, base, bonus, caps, status and cadence are untouched.
   `calculateOwnerEarningsGuaranteed` already `round2`s, so `decideOwnerGross`'s `round2` is a no-op for
   agree clips: not a repricing.

**This fix is provably SAFE and prospective:** all 108 ambiguous clips are old-window (created before the
